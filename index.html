Welcome

Hi, I’m the Adrift Yogi. I help climbers, surfers, hikers, and skiers build strength, mobility, and calm through yoga and breathwork—so you can send your projects and still have energy for life.

My teaching blends functional movement, evidence-informed practice, and traditional yoga. Expect grounded flows, breath-led pacing, and simple recovery tools you can use between work and the crag. Whether you’re aiming to level up your bouldering grade, prep for a surf trip, or just feel better day-to-day, I’ve got you.

Mobility & Strength

Progressive flows to support joint control, flexibility, and power for your sport.

Breathwork

Simple protocols for focus on the wall, calm before sleep, and recovery after effort.

Mindfulness

Practical tools to manage fear, build confidence, and enjoy the ride.
